About JKT48 Live Notification

JKT48 Live Notification is a Discord bot that provides real-time notifications for JKT48 live streams on Showroom and IDN Live. Stay updated with your favorite JKT48 members' live streams and never miss a moment!

All Commands

/whitelist

Menambahkan whitelist channel untuk live notifications.

/removewhitelist

Menghapus whitelist channel untuk live notifications.

/tagrole

Role akan otomatis tertag saat notifikasi member sedang live.

/removetagrole

Menghapus tag role saat notifikasi member sedang live.

All Permissions

View Channel

Agar bot bisa mengakses channel yang anda whitelist.

Send Message

Agar bot bisa mengirimkan Live Notification pada channel anda.

Embed Links

Agar bot bisa mengirimkan embed message pada channel anda.
